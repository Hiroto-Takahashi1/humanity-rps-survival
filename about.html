About This Site

Humanity Death Game: Rock-Paper-Scissors Survival is a simple browser game themed around the astronomically low odds of surviving. It lets you experience a one-shot, world-scale game of rock-paper-scissors — in other words, a “global-scale luck simulator.”

This site is intended purely for entertainment and is free for anyone to play. Game results and records are stored in your browser, allowing you to enjoy the journey toward becoming world champion as many times as you like.

※ This site is an unofficial, personal project. It has no connection whatsoever to any real individuals or organizations.
